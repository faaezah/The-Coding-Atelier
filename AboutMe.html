About The Coding Atelier

Faaezah is the founder of The Coding Atelier. While handing out food packages with her grandmother, she met a girl who dreamed of becoming a computer scientist. The girl believed her dream was impossible due to her family's financial situation. Thus, the Coding Atelier was born. The Coding Atelier—especially through our coding kits—shatters the myth that learning computer science requires a computer. We nurture children from underprivileged communities, giving them opportunities to grow and shine brighter.
