Faaezah — Founder

About Faaezah

Faaezah is the founder of The Coding Atelier, a student‑driven tech collective dedicated to making computer science feel creative, accessible, and community‑centered. As the captain of Robotics Team 7797, Vice President of Machine Learning, and a young innovator who built an AI learning model to help students understand complex topics more easily, she brings a rare blend of technical depth and design‑forward thinking.

Through The Coding Atelier, she aims to empower students worldwide with modern, visually engaging tech education, expand international ambassador networks, and create projects that prove young people can lead real change in the tech space. Her mission is simple: turn curiosity into capability, and capability into impact.

Global Ambassadors

Lucinda — Netherlands Ambassador

About Lucinda

Lucinda is a dedicated ambassador for The Coding Atelier, driven by her passion for technology and its ability to bridge educational gaps. After moving from London to South-Holland in the 5th grade, she became deeply interested in the logic of Arduino and hands-on computing.

She now channels that curiosity into facilitating the initiative’s screen-free workshops, helping underserved students transition from technology users to confident, capable creators. Lucinda is honored to support this youth-led movement and is eager to expand the program’s reach and impact in the years to come.

Aksharaa — United Kingdom Ambassador

About Aksharaa

Aksharaa serves as the British Ambassador for The Coding Atelier, bringing a thoughtful and community‑driven approach to expanding access to creative computing. Growing up in the UK’s rich academic environment, she developed a strong appreciation for how technology can empower young learners to think boldly and solve real-world problems.

She is passionate about helping students discover confidence through hands-on learning and is committed to supporting The Coding Atelier’s mission of making coding accessible, inclusive, and inspiring. Aksharaa looks forward to helping grow the initiative’s presence across the United Kingdom and uplifting the next generation of young innovators.
