A Journey of Passion and Creativity

Faaezah is a 14-year-old sophomore based in Texas whose passion for computer science and robotics runs deep. After moving from London to the U.S. in 8th grade, she found herself navigating a new world — and turned to coding as a way to connect with something meaningful. What began as self-taught experiments in Java and Python quickly evolved into a love for logic, creativity, and building things that matter.

Faaezah's roles in clubs and activities

As the software lead of her FTC Robotics team, Faaezah has spent countless hours coding, debugging, and solving real-world challenges. One of her proudest moments was fixing a robot’s path-shaking issue — a breakthrough that reminded her how powerful persistence and precision can be. She’s now in her second year of robotics, mentoring younger teammates and leading software development with confidence and care. Outside of robotics, Faaezah has conducted a study on which encryption tool is the best after a nationwide ssn data breach, taught Java to peers, and taken advanced electives in cybersecurity and computer science. Her work reflects a belief that technology should be inclusive, empowering, and deeply human.

Join the Journey

The Coding Atelier is Faaezah’s way of turning passion into purpose. Through robotics, teaching, and community outreach, she’s building a future where every child — regardless of background — feels seen, capable, and inspired to dream in code. This is just the beginning, and the journey ahead is filled with heart, grit, and boundless possibility.
