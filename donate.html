Our Belief

At The Coding Atelier by Faaezah, we believe every child deserves the chance to dream in code. Across India and the United States, countless children grow up without access to computers — not because they lack talent or curiosity, but because opportunity never reached them. We’re here to change that.

How We Build

Our CodeKits aren’t simple handouts — they’re carefully crafted learning systems. Each kit is tiered to meet students where they are, offering both offline and online tools, hands-on exercises, and real software they can use to build, explore, and grow. We refurbish laptops, design immersive workshops, and deliver resources that spark creativity, confidence, and long-term digital literacy.

What We Stand For

We’re not just teaching technology — we’re building bridges between privilege and potential, between isolation and opportunity.

Why Your Gift Matters

Your donation isn’t just money. It’s a child’s first keyboard. Their first real project. Their first moment of being seen. Help us light up libraries in Austin and orphanages in India. Help us deliver hope, dignity, and opportunity — one CodeKit, one child, one future at a time. Donate today. Be the reason a child believes they belong.
