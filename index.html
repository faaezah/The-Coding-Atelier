Mission Statement

The Coding Atelier by Faaezah aims to empower underprivileged children in Austin and India with digital literacy by refurbishing discarded laptops, creating screen-free beginner-friendly code kits, and leading hands-on computing workshops.

The Power of Possibility

Technology isn’t just a tool — it’s a gateway to imagination. For children who’ve never had access, even a single moment with a computer can change how they see the world and themselves. At The Coding Atelier, we believe in nurturing that spark — giving kids the space to explore, create, and feel proud of what they build.

Crafted For Curiousity

Every CodeKit is designed like a story — layered, intentional, and hands-on. Whether it’s unplugged activities or USB-based software, each piece invites children to learn through play, discovery, and real-world connection. We don’t just teach code. We craft experiences that feel personal, joyful, and empowering.
